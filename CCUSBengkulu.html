Empowering Youth for a Sustainable Future

Bridging cultures and building environmental consciousness. CCUS supports Indonesian youth in pursuing international education while championing carbon footprint awareness for a greener planet.

Our Dual Mission

Environmental Education

Creating eco-conscious global citizens

We educate young individuals on managing their carbon footprint through seminars, workshops, and interactive programs that highlight the importance of environmental sustainability in daily life.

International Education Support

Opening doors to global opportunities

We support Indonesian youth in pursuing studies in Russia by providing cross-cultural approaches that facilitate international adaptation, collaboration, and academic success.

Why Carbon Footprint Awareness Matters

The choices we make daily significantly impact our planet. By understanding and managing our carbon footprint, we can collectively work towards environmental preservation while pursuing educational excellence across borders.
